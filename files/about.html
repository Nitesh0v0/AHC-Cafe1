Our Story

From Garage to Café

It started with a beat-up vintage roaster, two obsessive coffee lovers, and a garage in Bhairahawa later joining with three more visionaries. We spent a year sourcing, failing, and perfecting before we opened our doors to the public in 2025.

Today, After Hours Cafe and Resto is a community hub — a place where laptops meet lattes, where first dates happen over flat whites, and where regulars know their baristas by name.

Every single bean we use is sourced through direct-trade partnerships with smallholder farms we visit personally. When you drink our coffee, you're tasting a relationship.
